Introduction

We provide an executive summary of the analysis and suggestions made in reaction to the Unit 3 assessment report. The report aims to assess the organization's current condition and identify areas for improvement in marketing tactics, financial results, and operational effectiveness. It offers a comprehensive analysis of the findings and practical recommendations to address issues and seize opportunities. The analysis and recommendations were provided by a team of subject-matter experts.

Findings

  • The company's sales success over the previous year has been below average. It needs to evaluate its sales process and implement strategies to increase sales success.
  • The business' marketing strategies have not successfully reached the target market, resulting in a poor conversion rate. Customized campaigns addressing the target market's needs and preferences should be created.
  • Customer complaints about customer care and product quality have led to unfavorable reviews and a drop in customer retention. Improvements in customer service and product quality are required.
  • The company's data protection procedures do not meet GDPR requirements, risking legal obligations, reputation, and customer trust. Compliance strategies and security measures should be implemented.

Recommendations

  1. Appoint a Data Protection Officer (DPO) to oversee GDPR adherence and implement data security measures.
  2. Create a comprehensive data protection policy and ensure staff members are aware of and adhere to it.
  3. Implement organizational and technological safeguards to protect customer data, such as encryption and access controls.
  4. Provide regular training and education for employees on data protection and GDPR requirements.
  5. Conduct routine audits and assessments to evaluate the effectiveness of data protection measures and identify vulnerabilities.
